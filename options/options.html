User Manual

Hi there! Welcome to RadPin. This is an extension that will help your pin experience a lot faster and smoother.
Before you started, make sure you are log in to your pinterest account.

Instructions steps by steps

1. If it is your first time here OR you have new boards added on to your account, please enter your pinterest ID to the search input above.
2. Your boards will be displayed in the dropdown list. Pick the boards that you want to be associated with each key.
3. You can have up to three keys in each option. Pick the key combination you desire and then save your options.
4. Now you are ready to pin! Use your mouse to hover over the pin image and then press on the key. Viola! You don't believe it? Check your board!
